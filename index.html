Tribute to Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is a retired Indian cricketer who is widely considered as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.Famously known as "GOD OF CRICKET"

Achievements

  • Holds the record for most runs scored in both Test and One Day International cricket
  • Holds the record for most centuries in both Test and One Day International cricket
  • Scored 100 international centuries in his career
  • Recipient of the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award

Legacy

Tendulkar's legacy is that of a cricketer who inspired generations of Indian cricketers and fans alike. He paved the way for India to become a dominant force in international cricket and was instrumental in winning several key matches for India.
